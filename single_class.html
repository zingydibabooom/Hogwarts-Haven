Single Classic

Welcome to your cozy hideaway! Our Single Classic rooms are perfect for students who want a comfortable, private space to relax and study after a long day of classes. The vintage furniture and high ceilings give the room a classic feel, while the modern touches ensure you have everything you need. The shared kitchen is spacious and perfect for whipping up a quick meal with your fellow housemates.

What's included in your Single Classic plan?
You will have your own room and share a kitchen and bathroom between 2-4 students.
- Shared hallway and bathroom featuring clawfoot bath
- Shared kitchen with modern amenities such as a double door refridgerator, combination stovetop/oven/microwave, coffee machine, and dining table
- Vintage furniture in your room: desk and chair, dresser, standing mirror, single bed, and armchair
- Gym and laundry room access
