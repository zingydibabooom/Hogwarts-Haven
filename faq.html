The process of booking a room is straightforward. If you are not yet registered, simply navigate to the "Rooms" section on the website, select the room type you wish to book, and proceed with the "Book Now!" option. You will then be prompted to register on the website and provide some basic information. After that, you will be taken to the "Selection Info" page, followed by the "Application form" and "Payment" section.

If you have already registered, you can follow the same process as above, but you won't be prompted to register again when you click the "Book Now!" option.

Completing your booking is a simple process that can be done in just a few easy steps. It will be helpful to have some information ready before you begin, such as the name, email, and address of your guarantor, as well as a bank card for payment of the advance rent or booking fee (for ROI only). Once you have chosen your room, you can expect to complete the booking process in no more than 16 minutes.
