Web Accessibility Policy

Goals

At Hogwarts Haven, we are committed to providing a website that is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We believe in creating an inclusive digital environment that ensures equal access to our services and information. Our goal is to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA to ensure that our website is usable and accessible to the widest possible audience. We understand that web accessibility is not only a moral obligation but also a legal requirement in many countries. By complying with these regulations, we are taking steps to avoid legal risks and potential lawsuits. Additionally, we recognize that web accessibility is a social responsibility, thereby we ensure that all individuals can access information and services without any discrimination.

Practices

To achieve our accessibility goals, we have established the following policies and practices:

  • Our team evaluates and test the website from time to time for accessibility using tools such as WAVE (Web Accessibility Evaluation Tool). New features and changes on the website are then updated every first week of the month and are included in this policy.
  • We provide text alternatives, captions, and transcripts for non-text content and multimedia.
  • Our website uses semantic HTML tags in sequential orders to be supportive with assistive technologies and keyboards.
  • One of our goals is to ensure logical tab order throughout our website, including links, form controls, and interactive objects.
  • Our website has sufficient color contrast to enhance readability.
  • The website does not automatically play audio upon loading. In cases where audio is present, users can easily disable it or adjust the volume through provided controls.
  • Links on our website are easily recognizable without relying on colors alone, both in their default and hover states, to ensure accessibility for all users.
  • We are trying to make our website robust and compatible with various devices and platforms, the work which is still under progress.
  • We strive to make our website easily comprehensible by utilizing straightforward language and supplying appropriate labeling and instructions.
  • ARIA attributes are used to label links, buttons, form and other HTML elements to make the website more compatible with assistive technologies.
  • This accessibility statement and contact information provided below are readily available to users who encounter any accessibility issues. This can be accessed from the footer section of our website.
  • We ensure that any third-party vendors who provide services on our website adhere to established accessibility standards and guidelines of WCAG 2.1.

By implementing these policies and practices, we strive to create an inclusive and accessible digital experience for all our users. We recognize that digital accessibility is an ongoing process and we are committed to continuously improving our website to meet the evolving needs of our users.
